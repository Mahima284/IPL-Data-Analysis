\# IPL Data Analysis (2008-2024)



\## Overview

Analysis of 1,090 IPL matches spanning 16 years to identify top performers and key patterns in batsman performance, bowler effectiveness, team strategy, and toss impact.



\## Dataset

\- 1,090 complete IPL matches from 2008 to 2024

\- 260,920 ball-by-ball delivery records

\- 19 IPL teams across 16 seasons

\- Source: Kaggle IPL Complete Dataset (2008-2024)



\## Analysis \& Findings



\### Batsman Analysis

Top 10 batsmen by strike rate show PD Salt leading with 169.6 strike rate, followed by TM Head (168.6) and AD Russell (164.2), indicating these batsmen are the most aggressive scorers in IPL history, scoring nearly 170 runs per 100 balls faced.



\### Bowler Analysis

Top 10 bowlers by economy rate display Sohail Tanvir as most economical bowler (6.23 runs/over), followed by A Chandila (6.28) and FH Edwards (6.40), proving these bowlers have exceptional control and give away fewest runs in the tournament.



\### Team Performance Analysis

Team win percentages reveal Lucknow Super Giants leading with 72.73% wins, Chennai Super Kings at 58.59%, and Mumbai Indians at 56.91%, showing these franchises are most dominant and consistent winners in IPL.



\### Toss Impact Analysis

Toss analysis demonstrates Rising Pune Supergiant winning 83.33% after toss, Gujarat Lions 66.67%, and Gujarat Titans 63.64%, proving that winning the coin toss provides significant advantage with 50-83% win probability depending on team strength.



\## Visualizations

Four comprehensive charts included showing top batsmen, bowlers, team performance, and toss impact analysis.

\## Key Insights

1\. Strike rate over 150+ is exceptional for batsmen in IPL

2\. Economy rate under 6.5 is excellent for bowlers

3\. Newer franchises show higher win percentages

4\. Winning toss increases win probability by 50-83%

5\. Team strength matters more than toss advantage alone
